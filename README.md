> **A premium, modern student productivity portal for managing tasks, courses, and academic life with style.**

TaskManager is more than just a list—it's your academic command center. Designed with a sleek "University Glass" aesthetic, it features real-time cloud syncing and a secure login that ensures only students can access their personalized dashboard.

---

## ✨ Why TaskManager?

### 🎓 Built for Students
- **University Restricted**: Only your institutional email can join, keeping your workspace professional and focused.
- **Smart Courses**: Track your subjects, instructors, and academic progress at a glance.
- **Interactive Schedule**: Never miss a lecture with a beautiful, real-time timeline of your day.

### 🎨 Stunning Visuals
- **Glassmorphism UI**: High-end frosted glass cards and transparent sidebars for a premium feel.
- **Dynamic Themes**: Seamlessly switch between a deep, cinematic Dark Mode and a crisp, clean Light Mode.
- **Fluid Animations**: Every interaction feels smooth and alive with subtle micro-animations.

### ☁️ Modern & Reliable
- **Cloud Powered**: Your data is always safe and synced in real-time across all your devices using Firebase.
- **Privacy First**: Personalized dashboards ensuring your academic data remains yours and yours alone.

---

## 🛠️ Performance & Tech
- **Core Engine**: React 19 + Vite (Fast & Lightweight)
- **Cloud Backend**: Firebase Auth & Firestore
- **Icon Library**: Lucide React
- **Design System**: Vanilla CSS Variables (Premium Dark/Light Themes)

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign In**: Use your university email to access your workspace.
2. **Setup Courses**: Add your current semester subjects.
3. **Plan Your Day**: Sync your daily schedule and start checking off tasks!
